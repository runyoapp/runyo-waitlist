Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

runyo respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it. We keep it short and clear.

1. Who we are

runyo is a personal training app for runners, built in Utrecht, the Netherlands. Questions about this policy? Email us at info@runyo.app.

2. What data we collect

We only collect what is needed to make the app work:

  • Email address - via the waitlist, so we can let you know when runyo goes live.
  • Google account details - name and email address via Google OAuth, so you can sign in to the app. We do not store passwords.
  • Google Sheets data - your training plan, which you connect yourself. We only read it to make the app work and do not store it on our servers.
  • Notification settings - times and preferences for Telegram notifications, so the bot knows when to send you a message.

3. What we use your data for

  • To let you know when runyo is publicly available (waitlist).
  • To give you access to the app via Google OAuth.
  • To send daily training notifications via Telegram, if you set that up.
  • We do not sell data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. How long we keep your data

  • Waitlist email addresses are kept until runyo goes live and we have informed everyone, or as soon as you unsubscribe.
  • App settings are kept as long as you have an account. You can delete your data at any time by contacting us at info@runyo.app.

5. Scopes we use via Google OAuth

When you sign in with Google, we request access to:

  • Basic profile details (name, email address) - for identification.
  • Google Spreadsheets - to read and update your training plan.

We never request more access than is needed for the app to work.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we keep about you.
  • Have incorrect data corrected.
  • Have your data deleted.
  • Object to processing.

Email us at info@runyo.app and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and tracking

runyo uses no tracking cookies, no advertising networks, and no third-party analytics services that follow your behavior.

8. Changes

If we change this policy, we update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you by email.
